How does the Visia engine differ from standard models?

Visia is not a wrapper for open-source models; it is a proprietary diffusion architecture engineered for semantic precision. It translates abstract creative intent into editorial-grade signal without the artifacts or hallucinations common in public datasets.

Do I retain full commercial rights to the output?

Absolute ownership. You retain 100% commercial and intellectual property rights to every asset generated. Our infrastructure acts solely as the computational conduit; the output is your property, free from royalties or attribution requirements.

How is data privacy handled for unreleased campaigns?

We operate on a strict "Zero-Retention" protocol for private generations. Your inputs, reference images, and outputs are isolated within your instance and are never indexed, shared, or used to train our public models.

What are the maximum export parameters?

Assets are delivered as uncompressed, high-fidelity files up to 8K resolution. We support multi-layer exports that integrate seamlessly into high-end post-production workflows (Adobe Creative Cloud, DaVinci Resolve, or Nuke).

Can Visia integrate into existing agency pipelines?

Yes. The Global tier grants full API access, allowing you to embed Visia’s generation engine directly into your CMS or proprietary internal tools for automated, high-volume asset scaling.

The price point is significantly higher than consumer AI tools; what justifies the investment?

Visia is engineered for the 1%. Unlike consumer tools optimized for novelty, Visia provides predictable, high-stakes reliability and a dedicated GPU environment that ensures zero queue latency. You are not paying for a subscription; you are investing in a closed-loop ecosystem that eliminates legal ambiguity, ensures enterprise-level security, and delivers a caliber of "Editorial Signal" that replaces costly multi-day physical productions.

Is advanced technical prompting required?

No. The "Signal" phase interprets natural language intent. While granular control is available for technical directors, the system is engineered to bridge the gap between abstract thought and concrete reality without complex syntax.
